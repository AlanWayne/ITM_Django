About us



A Timeless Passion for Precision

At OWS, we believe that time is not just a measure but a story. Our passion lies in crafting watches that not only tell time but also weave narratives of elegance, precision, and innovation. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to bringing the finest timepieces to discerning customers around the world.


The Journey of Excellence

Our journey began in 1891, rooted in a deep appreciation for horology and a vision to redefine the standards of watchmaking. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a globally recognized brand, celebrated for our meticulous craftsmanship, unique designs, and unwavering commitment to quality.


Crafted with Care

Each watch that leaves our workshops is a testament to our dedication. We draw inspiration from the timeless aesthetics of vintage watches, blending them with contemporary technology to create pieces that are as functional as they are beautiful. Our team of master craftsmen and women work tirelessly to ensure that every detail, from the intricate dials to the durable straps, meets our exacting standards.
